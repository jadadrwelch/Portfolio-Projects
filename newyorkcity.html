New York City

Quick Facts

\
  • As of July 2023 the population of New York City is 8,285,035 citizens
  • The Dutch West India Companydirected a settlement to be formed in lower East Manhattan in 1625, but it wasn't until February 2,1653 that the Dutch West India Company formed the first Common Council to represent the residents of the colony.
  • New York City is apart of the nation's metropolitan region along with other counties in states such as New Jersey and Connecticut
  • The New York City area along with other states and cities such as New Jersey and Newark is one of the most populous urban cities.
  • Median household income as of 2023 is $79,713 as opposed to the median income for the rest of the state at $84,578

Did You Know?

New York City is known as the "Big Apple" and is famous for its iconic skyline, Broadway shows, and diverse culture.
