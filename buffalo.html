Buffalo

Quick Facts

\
  • As of July 2023, the population of Buffalo is 274,678 citizens
  • The act to incorporate the city of Buffalo was passed by New York Legislature in April 1832
  • Buffalo is apart of the Western NY region, also known as Region 5
  • Generally considered urban, being the second largest city is New York. But it also offers suburban living.
  • As of July 2023, the median household income is $48,050
