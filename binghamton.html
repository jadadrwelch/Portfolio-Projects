Binghamton

Quick Facts

\
  • As of July 2023, the population is 46,272 citizens
  • The village was established in 1798 but was not incorporated until 1834
  • Located in the Southern Tier region of New York state near the Pennsylvania border
  • Binghamton is categorized as a dense suburban community
  • As of July 2023, the median household income is $44,331

Popular Foods in Binghamton

  1. Spiedies, which are marinated cubs of meat cooked on a skewer
  2. Italian Cuisine, such as pizza, pasta, and seafood pastas
